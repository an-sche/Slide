# Overview

## Elevator Pitch
The player controlls a unit that they try to navigate through difficult mazes and puzzels. There are unique surfaces they move on that make directing their character difficult but fun. As a team, players can help each other out by resurrecting their friends or opening doors.

## Genre
Puzzle platformer

## Core Loop
The gameplay loop is that the player is constantly making micro adjustments to their unit's movement. Moving around is simple to do but hard to master. They have to dodge obstacles and enemies at the same time. 

## Target Platform
PC

## Target Audience
RTS style enjoyers

## Unique Selling Points
- Teamwork makes the dreamwork. Without friends to help you when you crash, you will have a very difficult time beating the levels.
- Sprite-based characters (circles/arrows for prototype, full sprites as end goal). Each player gets a distinct color for multiplayer readability.

## Art Style
Prototype: simple geometric shapes — circles for units, arrows to indicate facing direction, solid color per player.
End goal: sprite-based characters with animations (idle, moving, dead).
